Protable Monitor

OTS Project for ViewSonic

Story

When monitor industry became full. All the company started to open new battle field. Though the research we found out a protable monitor like tablet can be the next generation.

So this monitor is the frist extended project we prepose to customer.

Project Goals

  • Helping company develop new product of monitor.
  • Using Mind Map and other user research method to discover other function of second protable monitor in modern life.
  • Provide a protable monitor suit for business customer.

Product Scenario

After years of display development,people not only use PC or NB.

There are many entertainment equipment beside us like Iphone, Ipad, Switch.The same requirement they might need is a extend display or bigger one.

And the environment user might use in coffee shop,public transportation or office with their NB.

This pircture showing three main point portable display is better than a tablet.Because customer probably think why they want to buy a protable display rather than a IPad.

So we considered the situation we need in business,conclude into "Convenient" , "Diversity" , "Privacy".

Embedded Stand

After brainstoming those idea,or sale team found us a client who want to lunch a nomal protable monitor.
Looking to get the order we suggest them embedded the stand into rearcover than just tell user to buy a plastic cover stand.

Base on the competitveness with other,this product have to be thin and cheep.We use thin plastic combine with a metal board .

According to the following picture,the stand can easliy open by using finger. We put stand on right side because sometimes user might turn the monitor into vartical.

From this two pictures,you can see more detail of the monitor,we erase mutiple cable into just Type-C,which connect with other devices. Power is also come with signal from the main device.
