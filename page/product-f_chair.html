Furniture Design

Design made by Story

Story

Over ten years ago, my family and I went to vacation in a mountain.The weather turned bad suddenly and started raining.
We wanted to go back,but in the path to the cab. A big Rock and wood drop down from the top of mountain,and just a little bit infront of me.

It was a unforgetable memory that I want to tell other.

Project Goals

  • Make a Product that come from a story.
  • A useful product for my house.

Product Scenario

This project was using a story that we experience before and change it into design element to make a product.

I used cement and metal to make a stool.The structure of this stool may let people feel unsafty to sit on it,but that is the effect I want.
Welding metal to be trunk for imagine and a function for hanging clothes.

Base on the effect I want,so even I dont want to sit on this stool.So I gave more useful funciton on it.

There was a stage can put box and let user throw little stuff like keychain after get home.

Not only those practical features.You can also planting on the top of cement,because I survived from that disaster. At least I can have new life.
