Story

When public transportation turns green, biking becomes popular around cities. It is exhausted and inconvenient when parents walk long distance with a stroller. If the stroller can combine withbikes or turn into a baby seat for bike, you can enjoy the journey as you desired.Imagining riding bike from your house to the park but the bicycles are prohibited in the public area, all you need to do is put down the children seat then you can take the bike in and enjoy the parent-children time together.

If you are really exhausted after shopping, just pull up the baby cart when passing the public bicycle stop then you can simply ride it home, and as well as other situations.​
