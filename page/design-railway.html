Taiwan Railway
Brand & Product Redesign

Redesign Project in Graduate school

Story

This project was the one I did in graduate school.Picking a local brand that really need to be reflesh.

I cooperated with different design field people.Rebuliding the brand from Brand Core Value to product even interior.

Project Goals

  • Redesign Taiwan Railway company's inside value and the image for public.
  • Redesign Taiwan Railway company's logo , ticket , website and other product.

Brand Inside Value

Before we did all the design for the company. We must set up their inside value for designer having idea to do brainstroming.In the class we learned difference between brand vision , core value , brand positioning.

Taiwan Railway's image to public is being late frequently, and the attitude of solving this isn't very appreciate by public. Changing this are the frist step they should begin right now.

There is another issue we found out, Taiwan Railway need a position between high speed railway and bus. We though they can focus on the local tourism. Providing different package for North , Middle and South each season.

Every brand design have their own patten, we wanted to pass "Empathetic" , "understanding" to other, changing R into much softer.

Using line only to illustrator this patten.It won't being abrupt to be some design's background (You can see in the following picture.).

We did lots of design, separate different kind of train into the different color. The color also can use on the sign in the station.Everyone who live in Taiwan know the sign are a mess in station.So we took train to 5 station surveing environment,and make new design for them.
