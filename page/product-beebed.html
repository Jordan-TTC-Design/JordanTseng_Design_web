Product by Jordan Tseng

Bee Bed

Accompany Furniture in hospital

Story

A furniture which is design for people who need to accompany their Hospitalize family.

This project main concept is to force student to think with the environment. By using design thinking and process like persona , I keep sculpture the shape and function until it really suit the jeremy map.
Hope this kind of process can be done in every design school.

Project Goals

  • User-centered design that meet the need of people in hospital.
  • Environment research in design target.

Extend 4 Different Mode

This project is come from the experience I took care my grandpa in the hospital when he had a surgery.

People must take care of someone when he suffering from serious illness,but everyone have their own life. So I conclude what kind of people like father,mother with two kid or students might need in the period.

I found out the biggest problem is they couldn't sleep because the accompany chair is too small and hard for them to rest.That was the reason I started this design.

Base on the ward is too small, the frist step I think was storage function.Nurse can flod it when nobody need this chair, and it also save the space in the storespace.

Each package include three boxs, after open it,the structure is string enough for adult sleep on it.

There are two issue I found out in the research, people might need a desk for work,and usually friends or family will bring somethings when they visiting patient.

In this moment,this chair provide three space for storage.Thief can't steal it when people sleep on their bed.
